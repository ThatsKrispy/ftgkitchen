The FTG Path

How we help you
grow your food business.

01

Get licensed & compliant

We walk you through FDA and DBPR licensing requirements. Our facility is fully compliant — your operation is covered from day one.

02

Build your production workflow

Use professional equipment and shared space to develop your menu, test recipes, and build a repeatable production process without heavy overhead.

03

Launch and find your first customers

Go live on delivery platforms, at markets, or through catering. We help you establish a foothold in South Florida's competitive food market.

04

Scale with confidence

As demand grows, add hours, storage, and staff without changing facilities. Grow into your own space when you're truly ready.
